What happens with your clothes?

How it works, step by step!

Step 1

Separate into types of item

Ensure that clothes are kept separate from other textile items.

Items NOT Accepted: Mattresses, Couch Cushions, Lawn Furniture Cushions, Foam Products (Mattress Pads, etc),Rugs and Toys

Step 2

Clean and dry

Please ensure that your textiles are cleaned properly and completely dry.

Step 3

Nearest Pod

Locate your nearest Pod. Find its location by clicking HERE

Step 4

Place in Pod

Place the clothes to be recycled into any Pod you like or schedule a pickup.

Step 5

Separated and transported

The clothes are sorted and transported to dedicated stations for reuse and recycling.
