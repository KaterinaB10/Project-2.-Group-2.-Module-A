What happens with your clothes?

Step 1

Seperate

Ensure that textiles are kept separate from toys and other miscellaneous items.

Step 2

Clean and dry

Please ensure that your textiles are properly cleaned and completely dry.

Step 3

Nearest Pod

Locate your closest Pod. Discover its location by clicking HERE

Step 4

Place in Pod

Place your belongings into the designated Pod or schedule a pickup.

Step 5

Separated and transported

The clothes are sorted and transported to dedicated stations for reuse and recycling.
